Transfer a session before creating a new PIN code

  1. Click Show Advanced Options in the Console window.
  2. Click Change to select the queue for the request.

  3. Select a Department or Technician, and click OK.

The request is sent to the selected technician or department.

What do you want to do?

  • Transfer before accepting a new support request
  • Transfer or share a support session with another technician
  • Provide assistance to your customers
