Take Control: How to generate applets for a specific technician/department

Last Modified

Tue May 05 13:57 GMT 2020

Description

  • How to generate applets for a specific technician.
  • How to generate applets for a specific department.

Environment

Solution

  1. Login to your Take Control Technician Console.
  2. Below the "START NEW SESSION" button, click Show Advanced Options.
  3. Click the CHANGE... button.
  4. Select the department/technician you which to receive the request(s).
    • Ensure the technician is properly configured to receive such requests as per the article [%=General.ProductName_tc_long%]: Support Request Visibility for Take Control Technicians.
  5. Click OK.
  6. Click START NEW SESSION.
  7. A support link/PIN code will be generate for an applet directed to the specific Technician/Department.
