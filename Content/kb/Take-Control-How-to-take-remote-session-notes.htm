Take Control: How to take remote session notes

Last Modified

Tue Feb 12 18:19 GMT 2019

Description

  • How to take remote session notes during a Take Control remote session.

Environment

  • /

Solution

  1. Perform a remote session to the intended device;
  2. Click the System Details/Request Details icon, located on the top right corner of your Viewer;
  3. Select the REQUEST DETAILS tab;
  4. Write down your notes and click the SAVE button;
  5. You may now consult your remote session notes on the Admin Area under Reports > Session History.
