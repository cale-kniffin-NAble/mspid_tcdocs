How to force a technician to change password in Take Control?

Last Modified

Tue May 26 23:34 GMT 2020

Description

  • Nee to force password change on a technician.
  • How to lockout a technician?

Environment

Solution

  • Please take into account these actions can only be done by a technician with Administrator privileges:
  1. Go to the Admin Area;
  2. Navigate to Management > Technicians;
  3. Select the intended technician;
  4. Under the TECHNICIAN tab (select by default) click the Apply button, located below the text "Lockout the technician and force password change";
  5. The technician shall receive and email to update his password, otherwise he won't be able to login with the old credentials.
