: How to check the Session History

Last Modified

Thu Nov 26 10:39 GMT 2020

Description

  • How to consult previously performed sessions (including session time, details and notes);

Environment

  • /

Solution

  • From the Admin Area:
  1. Login to your Admin Area;
  2. Navigate to Reports > Session History;
  3. The session history list displays the most recent sessions, use the Filters and Date: dropdowns to search for specific results;
  4. Review the resulting list and if desired check a detailed report for a singular session;
    1. Select a session from the list;
    2. Click the link Show in Reports Page (bottom right corner);
  • From the Take Control Technician Console:
  1. Open Take Control Technician Console;
  2. Click on the History menu;
  3. The console session history list displays the most recent sessions, use the SEARCH button to filter specific results.
  • There's a limit of 90 days or 5000 sessions to export session history data. If the period you want to export is wider than 3 months or 5000 sessions, you would have to export in blocks of 90 days or 5000 sessions each, instead of all at once.
