Take Control: How to start/stop remote session video recordings

Last Modified

Mon Jul 08 16:45 GMT 2019

Description

  • How to initiate a remote session recording;
  • How to terminate a remote session recording;

Environment

  • /
    • Take Control

Solution

  • During a remote session:
  1. Click the Video Recording Status icon, on the top right corner of the ;
  2. Afterwards, the icon will show red, indicating the session is being recorded;
  • To stop video recording, click the red icon again. It shall now appear as grey, indicating there's no ongoing session recording.
