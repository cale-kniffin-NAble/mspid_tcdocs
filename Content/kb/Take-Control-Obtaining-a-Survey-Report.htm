Take Control: Obtaining a Survey Report

Last Modified

Wed Nov 14 11:58 GMT 2018

Description

  • How to obtain a Survey Report?
  • Where can I review all answers to a survey?
  • Where can I review answers to a survey from a specific session?

Environment

  • /

Solution

  1. Login to your Admin Area;
  2. Navigate to Reports > Surveys
  3. Filter the results from the list using the filters above the list of surveys
  4. Click the intended survey from the list and a new tab will open to the right with that session's survey response
    • You can also select the Analytics or Export option for additional details on the selected survey results (or the entire list)
