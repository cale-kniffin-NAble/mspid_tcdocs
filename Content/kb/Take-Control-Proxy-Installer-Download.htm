Take Control: Proxy Installer Download

Last Modified

Wed Feb 13 14:29 GMT 2019

Description

  • Looking to allow devices with no connection to connect through a single device using the Take Control Proxy!
  • Where to download the Proxy installer?

Environment

  • /.

Solution

  1. Login to your Admin Area;
  2. Navigate to Management > Proxies;
  3. Click on the Add button near the top right corner;
  4. You will receive a prompt with port requirements in order to confirm your download request, please read through it carefully;
  5. Click on Download on the prompt to download the Proxy Installer.

Article Number: 113330

Internal notes: PLEASE NOTE: The device where the proxy will be installed still needs to meet the Take Control communication requirements: unfiltered outbound access on ports 80 and 443 to all *.pubnub.com, *.beanywhere.com, *.mspa.n-able.com and *.swi-tc.com domains!
