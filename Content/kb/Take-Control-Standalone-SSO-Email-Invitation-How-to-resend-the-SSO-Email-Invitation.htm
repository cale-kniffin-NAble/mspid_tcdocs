: SSO Email Invitation/How to resend the SSO Email Invitation

Last Modified

Fri Aug 28 12:09 GMT 2020

Description

  • All techs receiving an email asking to setup for Take Control - is this legitimate?
  • A tech has not received the SSO Invitation email, how can I resend it?

Environment

  • /.

Solution

  • All registered technicians existing prior to July 2018 should have received an email inviting them to migrate their login to .
  • These SSO invitation emails are legitimate and were sent out by our Engineering team to both inform and invite all technicians to make use of the feature.
  • If you cannot find the email, please resend them as follows:
    1. Go to your Admin Area;
    2. Navigate to Management>Technicians;
    3. Select the desired tech;
    4. Click the Send Invitation button, below the Single Sign On (SSO) text.
