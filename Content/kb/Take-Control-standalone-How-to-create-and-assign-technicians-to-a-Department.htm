: How to create and assign technicians to a Department

Last Modified

Tue May 05 14:06 GMT 2020

Description

  • How to create a new department on Take Control standalone;
  • How to assign technicians to a certain department.

Environment

  • /

Solution

  • To create a new Department:
  1. Open your Admin Area;
  2. Navigate to Management > Departments;
  3. Click the Add button (top right corner);
  4. Type the name of the Department;
  5. As soon as you click on Save, the new Department is created and it will show on the Departments list.
  • To assign technicians to a department:
  1. Open your Admin Area;
  2. Navigate to Management > Departments;
  3. Select the intended department from the Departments list;
  4. Click the TECHNICIANS tab;
  5. Choose the desired technicians from the drop-down;
  6. Click Save.
