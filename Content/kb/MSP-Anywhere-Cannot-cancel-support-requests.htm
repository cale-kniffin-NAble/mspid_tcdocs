: Cannot cancel support requests

Last Modified

Thu Jul 12 13:25 GMT 2018

Description

  • Technician cannot cancel support requests.
  • How to enable a technician to cancel remote support requests?

Environment

Solution

  1. Go to your Admin Area;
  2. Navigate to Management>Technicians;
  3. Select the desired technician;
  4. Choose PERMISSIONS>GLOBAL;
  5. Turn On the option Can cancel Requests;
  6. Click the SAVE button.
