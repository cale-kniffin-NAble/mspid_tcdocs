Take Control: Flash drive not appearing on the "Please select the removable drive:" drop down

Last Modified

Mon Apr 15 13:07 GMT 2019

Description

  • In the Take Control console installation process, the flash drive does not show on the "Please select the removable drive:" drop down;
  • Unable to choose the intended Flash drive to install the Take Control console.

Environment

  • /

Solution

  • Only removable drives will be shown on the drop down;
  • Check if your drive is being detected as a Local Drive (if so,it won't appear on the drop down as it's not a removable drive);
    1. Go to My Computer;
    2. Right-click the drive at hand;
    3. Click Properties;
    4. Check the drive type under the General tab.
