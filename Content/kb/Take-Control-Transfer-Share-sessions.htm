Take Control: Transfer/Share sessions

Last Modified

Thu Feb 14 13:54 GMT 2019

Description

  • Is it possible to share or transfer a session to another technician? How?

Environment

  • /;
  • Windows.

Solution

  1. On your session window click the Session Options icon near the top right (the icon appears as a downward facing arrow within a circle);
  2. Hover over the Session menu;
  3. Click the Transfer / Share option;
    • A window with the list of other technicians and departments in your company will appear.
  4. Select the intended Technician or Department;
  5. Click the TRANSFER TO... or INVITE TO JOIN... button as intended (with the former for transfers and the latter to share the session) to complete the task.
    • Note that this feature is currently only available through the Windows Console.
