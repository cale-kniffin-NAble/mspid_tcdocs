Take Control: How to add a Department logo on Applet Sessions

Last Modified

Tue May 05 14:12 GMT 2020

Description

  • How to add Department logo on applet sessions.
  • How to provide an specific department applet link.

Environment

Solution

  1. Navigate to your Admin Area;
  2. Go to Management > Departments;
  3. Choose the desired department from the Departments list;
  4. On the DEPARTMENT tab you can find the Department link and the Department logo;
  • To change the Department logo click the Change picture button.
