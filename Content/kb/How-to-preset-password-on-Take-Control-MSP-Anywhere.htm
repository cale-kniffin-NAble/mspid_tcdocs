How to preset a password on

Last Modified

Wed Aug 19 08:45 GMT 2020

Description

  • How to preset a password on

Environment

Solution

  1. Navigate to your Dashboard.
  2. Click Settings>Take Control>Policy.
  3. Hit New/Edit.
  4. Click on the Security tab.
  5. Under Authentication method choose Preset Password .
  6. Define your password.
  7. Save.
  8. Apply the policy on the desired devices.
