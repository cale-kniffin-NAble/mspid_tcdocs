Take Control: Lost bottom bar and General tab after updating to 7.00.00

Last Modified

Fri Feb 22 19:17 GMT 2019

Description

  • After updating the console to version 7.00.00, we can no longer find the General tab, are the options available on it still available?
  • After updating, sessions are no longer showing the bottom bar, are the options missing?
  • Where can I send commands from in the new Viewer? Where is the bottom bar?

Environment

  • /:
    • Tech Console version 7.00.00 or greater (Windows).
    • Tech Console version 6.00.00 or greater (macOS).
    • Take Control Viewer 7.00.00 or greater (Windows).
    • Take Control Viewer 6.00.00 or greater (macOS).
  • :
    • 7.00.00 or greater (Windows).
    • 6.00.00 or greater (macOS).

Solution

  • The update to version 7.00.00 included several UI changes, including the removal of the General Tab and the bottom menu bar - however, none of the options available in either were removed, simply relocated to existing or new menu icons in the top bar.
  • For an overview of these UI changes, see Release Notes.
