: Restart and Reconnect

Last Modified

Wed Aug 19 10:00 GMT 2020

Description

  • How to restart a remote computer during a session.
  • How to reconnect to a remote device after a reboot.

Environment

Solution

  1. Select the COMPUTER menu;
  2. Click Restart & Shutdown;
  3. Click the option Restart & Reconnect;
  4. The remote device will now shutdown, restart and pick up the session.
