Take Control: How to take screenshots during a remote support session

Last Modified

Wed Oct 09 02:27 GMT 2019

Description

  • This article instructs on how to take screenshots during a Take Control support session.

Environment

  • /
    • Take Control

Solution

  • During a remote support session:
  1. Click the Remote Desktop tab;
  2. Go to Interactions > Take Screenshot
    • Alternatively you can use the keyboard shortcut Shift + Ctrl + P.
  3. Choose where the screenshot will be saved into, enter the file name and choose the file type.
  4. Click Save.
