: Tech Console Inbox only showing opened tickets

Last Modified

Thu Mar 15 16:53 GMT 2018

Description

  • Tech Console Inbox only showing opened tickets;
  • How to show other tickets, besides opened tickets, on Tech Console?

Environment

Solution

  • Take into account the Inbox menu only shows opened tickets by default. To show other tickets follow the steps below:
    1. Login on your Tech Console Application;
    2. Click the Inbox menu located on your console's north pane;
    3. Click the SEARCH button;
    4. A window will popup. set Status to All States, In Progress or Closed as needed;
    5. Click the SEARCH button.
