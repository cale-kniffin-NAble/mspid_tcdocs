How to change the Take Control policy being applied to remote devices

Last Modified

Wed Jul 10 12:35 GMT 2019

Description

  • The current article addresses ways to change the Take Control policy being applied to remote devices;

Environment

Solution

  • By client/site:
  1. Login to your RMM dashboard;
  2. Navigate to Settings > Take Control > Settings;
  3. Click the + icon to populate Servers/Workstations by client and site;
  4. Select the Setting, Engine and Policy as desired;
  • By device:
  1. Double click the desired device;
  2. Select the Remote Access tab;
  3. Choose the desired policy under the Policy Settings area;
  4. Click OK.
