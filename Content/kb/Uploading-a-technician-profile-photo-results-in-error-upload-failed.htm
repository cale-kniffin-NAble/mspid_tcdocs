Uploading a technician profile photo results in error: upload failed

Last Modified

Tue Feb 27 13:32 GMT 2018

Description

  • Uploading a profile photo for a technician in 's Admin Area results in Error: "Upload failed".

Environment

Solution

  • A Technician must be selected from the Technician's list before trying to upload the photo in the Identification tab. Otherwise, the software doesn't know which Technician to assign the photo to and returns the "Upload failed." error.
