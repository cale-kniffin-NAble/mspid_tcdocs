: How to Change a Technician's Expiration Date

Last Modified

Thu Mar 19 13:17 GMT 2020

Description

  • Instructions on how to change a Technician's expiration date;
  • Unable to change a Technician's expiration date through the Admin Area;
  • Instructions on how to re-enable an expired technician;

Environment

  • /

Solution

  1. Clear your Browser's cache or proceed in a different browser;
  2. Login to your Admin Area;
  3. Go to Management;
  4. Click on Technicians and select the technician;
  5. Select a date on the Valid Until box or check Never expires;
  6. Click on Save;
NOTE: When the technician expires a red "Expired" label will appear on their profile, and the technician won't be able to login until a new expiration date is set.
