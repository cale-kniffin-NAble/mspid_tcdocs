Check the session recording repository

Last Modified

Wed Nov 30 21:44 GMT 2016

Description

This article details where you can check the session history and view the session recordings.

Environment

Solution

  1. Log in to the Admin Area or click on the Admin area link on the Tech console.
  2. Go to Reports > Session History and select the session.

  3. Upon clicking on the session, you can see the session recordings on the Video tab or on the Remote System where a transcript of when the recording started.
