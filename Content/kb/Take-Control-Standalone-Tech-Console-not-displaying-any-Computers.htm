: Tech Console not displaying any Computers

Last Modified

Wed 13 Jan 17:39 GMT 2021

Description

  • Tech Console does not to display Computers (Agents)
  • Unable to view any Agents under the My Computers tab (or even the tab itself)
  • My Computers tab missing on the Take Control Tech Console

Environment

  • /

Solution

  • Technician Permissions
    • The Technician may be lacking the required Permissions to view the My Computers tab or specific Computer groups
    • To correct this, please perform the following steps:
  1. Login to the Admin Area
    • Please note that you will need sufficient permissions to access the admin area, if you are not an admin - please reach out to your account's administrator
  2. In the Admin Area, navigate to Management > Technicians and select the Technician's profile
  3. Select the Permissions tab
  4. Be sure that under "My Computers Management" Permissions, Accessto My Computers is selected
    • Press the Select Groups button to choose which groups the Technician will have access to
    • The Root Group is always accessible
  5. Save when done
  • Account Status
    • The Take Control account may have expired, please consider reviewing if you have renewed your Take Control license/s and verify if you've received a "New Customer Activation" email containing your activation link for the Take Control account;
    • Should you not have received any email regarding activation and should your account be expired (as per the expiration date present in your Admin Area) please reach out to your account representative or renewal agent.

Article Number: 116627

Internal notes: If the partner's account is expired on the msp anywhere database while indicating that it should be active in the Salesforce and there is no valid activation email, please reach out to the Provisioning or msp anywhere support team, in the rare chance that the partner's expiration date will need to be manually corrected.
