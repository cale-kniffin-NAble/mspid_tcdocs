: during a remote support session viewer window always staying in the foreground

Last Modified

Fri Jun 08 17:49 GMT 2018

Description

  • The support session window always stays in the foreground unless minimized to the taskbar.
  • The always stays on top of other application windows that are open.

Environment

Solution

  • While on a remote session click the pin icon that says "Always on top", located on the top right corner of your viewer.
