Take Control: How to choose a URL to display to the customer when the Applet is closed

Last Modified

Thu Dec 06 17:35 GMT 2018

Description

  • How to choose a URL to display to the customer when the Applet is closed

Environment

  • /

Solution

  1. Navigate to your Admin Area;
  2. Go to Profile > Applet Setup;
  3. Under the Applet Setup zone enable the option Choose a URL to display to the customer when the Applet is closed;
  4. Insert the desired URL;
  5. Click Save.
