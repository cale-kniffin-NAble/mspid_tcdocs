: How to export Survey History data

Last Modified

Wed Oct 14 10:32 GMT 2020

Description

  • Is there any way to get Survey data that I can link to sessions?
  • I'd like to use Survey History results in my own app/website, where can I get this info?
  • Can I extract Survey History in a csv file?

Environment

  • .

Solution

  1. Login to the Admin Area;
  2. Navigate to Reports > Surveys;
  3. Filter for the intended survey results (upper left corner of the questionnaire list);
  4. Select Export and OnlySelected or Complete Results (as best suits the purpose), in the upper right corner;
  5. This will trigger a download of a CSV file with the data of the surveys and their answers, also including session ID, Date and Time, etc.
