Take Control: How to check chat transcript from the technician console?

Last Modified

Tue Oct 30 13:53 GMT 2018

Description

  • How to find remote session's chat through the tech console?

Environment

Solution

  1. Open the technician console;
  2. Click on the History menu;
  3. Select the session whose conversation you want to review;
  4. Scroll down the session details until you find the SHOW ALL button (lower right corner);
  5. Clicking on SHOW ALL new a window will open with the corresponding chat transcript.
