View scripts executed from at a given time

Last Modified

Wed Nov 30 21:44 GMT 2016

Description

This article provides steps on viewing scripts executed from at a given time

Environment

Solution

  1. Log in to the Admin Console - https://admin.corporate.beanywhere.com
  2. Go to Reports > Scripts Jobs and click Search.
  3. Identify the date coverage for your search, then click Search within the dialog box.
