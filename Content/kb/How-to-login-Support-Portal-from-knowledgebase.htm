How to login to Support Portal from Knowledgebase

Last Modified

Tue Jul 10 20:00 GMT 2018

Description

  • I am having issues logging into the support portal. I do not see the option to login when I go to the knowledge base.

Environment

  • Support Portal

Solution

  1. Hover mouse over Support dropdown
  2. Select Request Help
  3. Enter login credentials and select Log In
