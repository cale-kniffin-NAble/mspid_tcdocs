: Disable optimizations/Keep Wallpaper during remote sessions

Last Modified

Mon Nov 04 15:08 GMT 2019

Description

  • keeps removing the remote desktop background on connections, leaving it black/blue
  • How to disable removal of clear type fonts, background and aero effects?

Environment

Solution

  1. Go to the
  2. Click on Settings
  3. Click on Take Control
  4. Click on Policy
  5. Click on Edit or create a New policy
  6. Under Performance Settings disable the following option: Optimize desktop settings when in session
  7. Save the Policy
  8. Apply the Policy to the relevant devices and Run Checks on the devices (or wait until the next cycle) to ensure the new settings are applied
