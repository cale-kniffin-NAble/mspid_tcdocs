Solution

  • During your remote session, you can toggle the Viewer menus to automatically hide or display themselves as follows:
    1. If the menus are hidden, hover over the top of the desktop with your mouse pointer as this will trigger their temporary display;
    2. Left click on the Preferences menu (vertical ellipsis on the top right corner);
    3. Left click on Auto-hide bars in Remote Desktop;
    • Alternatively, should you have Viewer Keyboard Shortcuts enabled (which is their default state), try using the key combination Shift+Control+Enter to toggle this option off/on.
