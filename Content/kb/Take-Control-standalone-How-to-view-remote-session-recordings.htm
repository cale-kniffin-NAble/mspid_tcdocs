: How to view remote session recordings

Last Modified

Thu Aug 06 14:27 GMT 2020

Description

  • How to watch Take Control standalone session video recording(s)?

Environment

  • /

Solution

  • From the Tech Console:
  1. Open your Take Control Console application
  2. Click the History menu
  3. Click on the relevant session or search for it using the Search scope if it isn't listed
  4. Click the VIDEOS tab to check session recordings
  • From the Admin Area:
    1. Go to Reports > Session History
    2. Select the relevant session or search for it using the Filters located on the left side pane
    3. Click the Show in Reports Page hyperlink, located on the bottom right corner
    4. When you click on the session entry a new browser tab will open showing the session details, organized in tabs
    5. Click on Video (n), where n is the number of video segments for the session
    6. Click on a video segment's thumbnail to play it
    • You will also be able to get a link to share or download the recording by clicking the link icon and/or download icon respectively

Article Number: 115003

Internal notes: Videos are only available for 6 months, after that the customer must request a specific video for the intended session ID to be made available.
