Article Number: 112281

Internal notes: Customer success center does not display invoices/billing information. Customer accounts still use Community Portal to view/pay invoices online.Sometimes a customer will not be able to view their bills from here. In this case do the following:A success center user MUST have MSP Owner permissions in order to access the billing page.This comes with it sensitive data being available so we have to confirm this is going to a user that has the permission to have it.Contact account manager saying "This person needs access to usage reports, can we assign this to them or is there a trusted contact this can be assigned to?"Go to the user in SalesforceClick into the dropdown labelled "Manage External User" and click "View Customer User"Check the "Additional Details" section for "MSP - Owner" (if they have owner then they can access).To give them this, click "Edit" at the top of the pageUnder "Additional Details" use the dropdown for "Type" and change to "MSP - Owner"Save
