Take Control (All Versions): Take Control Sessions to HP devices keep dropping every few seconds

Last Modified

Thu May 30 10:45 GMT 2019

Description

  • As soon as you connect to an HP device the sessions keep dropping every few seconds.

Environment

  • /
  • Take Control

Solution

  • If the device has HP Velocity installed, you should uninstall it since is known that HP Velocity has a negative impact in Take Control.

Article Number: 110000
