Take Control: How to send a survey to a customer?

Last Modified

Wed May 29 11:00 GMT 2019

Description

  • This article contains instructions on how to send surveys to customers during a Take Control remote session.

Environment

Solution

  • During a remote support session:
  1. Click the arrow down icon on the right side of your Viewer's north pane;
  2. Select the option Session > Send Survey;
  3. Select the survey you wish to send to the customer;
  4. Click OK;
  • It's also possible to configure a survey to be sent automatically when the remote session ends, as follows:
  1. Login to the Admin Area;
  2. Select Management > Surveys;
  3. Select the intended survey;
  4. Under the SURVEY SETTINGS tab;
  5. Under the "Type" section, choose the option Mandatory;
  6. Click Save.
