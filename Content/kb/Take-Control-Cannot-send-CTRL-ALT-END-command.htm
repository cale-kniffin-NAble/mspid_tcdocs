Take Control: Cannot send CTRL-ALT-END command

Last Modified

Tue 12 Jan 17:35 GMT 2021

Description

  • Am I able to send a CTRL-ALT-END Command through a Take Control session?

Environment

  • Take Control
  • Windows

Solution

  • Take Control does NOT support sending commands that aren't listed in the Commands section (such as CTRL+ALT+END)

Article Number: 118188

Internal notes: DISREGARD VERSION 3Somehow misread article at CTRL-ALT-DEL and not CTRL-ALT-ENDMSP Anywhere DOES support the ability to send CTRL+ALT+DEL commands on Agent sessions through the COMMANDS option on the bottom right corner of the Viewer window (and also by use of the Keyboard Shortcut SHIFT+CTRL+DEL)MSP Anywhere DOESN'T support sending commands that aren't listed in the Commands section (such as CTRL+ALT+END as is used by certain third party access software)
