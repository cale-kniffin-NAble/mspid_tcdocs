: Only one technician can login despite having more than one license

Last Modified

Mon Dec 03 14:32 GMT 2018

Description

  • Your organization has subscribed to more than one Take Control (Technician) license
  • Only one of the technicians can login at a time

Environment

Solution

  • There are two likely causes for this:
    • The licenses have expired and haven't been renewed automatically for some reason. A single technician is able to login during a grace period of 7 days after the licenses expired.
This can be checked by:
  1. Logging in to the product's Admin Area
  2. Clicking on the name of the Tech on the top right corner of the screen
  3. Clicking on Licensing Information
  4. Checking the information on Renewal date for your Services: to know the number of licenses available and Number of Concurrent Licenses: in order to know their expiration date
    • If the licenses are expired please reach out to your Account Manager or to Tech Support to determine the cause of the failed renewal.
  • There may be some technician(s) logged in that you are not aware of, using some of the available licenses. To determine who is logged in to the product and using available licenses, if you are an Administrator please login to the product's Admin Area and then:
  • On Running Sessions under the Technician section will list all logged on Technicians that are using available licenses. You can use the Force Logout button in front of each Technician details to free up the corresponding license. This will not terminate their on-going remote sessions, only prevent them from transferring/pausing/sharing the on-going sessions as well as use any of the Console's functionalities.
  • If you're not an Administrator, you may still be able to see who is logged in depending on the Permissions assigned to you and the remaining technicians, namely the permissions "Can disconnect..." and "Can be disconnected".
    • In that case, if no licenses are available when you try to login to the Tech Console, a list will be displayed of all the technicians that have been assigned the "Can be disconnected" permission so that you can then force logout them and login to the software yourself.
  • Note that these features are only available for licenses.
