Take Control: Adding notes after a session has ended

Last Modified

Wed Feb 13 13:06 GMT 2019

Description

  • Can I add notes to Sessions after they have been closed? How?

Environment

  • /.

Solution

  1. Login to your Admin Area;
    • Note that you will need to login as a technician with sufficient privileges to view and edit Reports, particularly Session History.
  2. Navigate to Reports > Session History;
  3. Select the session from the list by clicking on the session;
    • You may need to use the Filters available on the top left corner to filter for the intended result.
    • Note that you should click on the session and not the checkbox to the left of it.
  4. On the right-hand pane, click on Show in Reports Page;
  5. On the newly opened Reports Page, select the CUSTOMER tab and write in the Notes text field;
  6. Click on Save (bottom-right corner) when done.
