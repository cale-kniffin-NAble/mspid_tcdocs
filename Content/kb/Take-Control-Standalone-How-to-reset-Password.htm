: How to reset Password

Last Modified

Thu Apr 25 10:54 GMT 2019

Description

  • How I can reset my password as the Main Admin?
  • How I can reset my password as a Technician?
  • Forgot password?

Environment

  • /

Solution

  1. Access the Admin Area login page;
  2. Click the Can't Access your Account? link;
  3. Input your username when prompted and press Send;
  4. Should you have already associated your Take Control login with , you'll be directed to the relevant Password Reset page, confirm the username there and press RESETYOURPASSWORD;
    • Otherwise, you will receive an email requesting your account enrollment on .
  5. You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password.
    • Check your spam/junk filter for the password reset email if you don't receive it within a few minutes.
