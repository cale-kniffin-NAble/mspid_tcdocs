Take Control: Is it possible to recover an agent or group of agents after deleting them?

Last Modified

Mon Nov 19 13:43 GMT 2018

Description

  • Is it possible to recover a group of agents after deleting them and the agents within the group?

Environment

  • /.

Solution

  • Once an agent is deleted it is not possible to recover itas from the moment the option "Delete" is selected on the drop down and the Delete button is pressed, the Agent will be effectively deleted from the device list and the uninstall command will be sent to the remote device.
