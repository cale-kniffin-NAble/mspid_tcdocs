Take Control: How to edit customer details for all devices under the same group

Last Modified

Wed Mar 27 16:48 GMT 2019

Description

  • How to mass edit customer details for all devices under the same group

Environment

Solution

  1. Login to your Admin Area;
  2. Navigate to the Devices menu;
  3. Click on the intended device group;
  4. On the chart below, under the GENERAL tab (opened by default) fill the Customer Email, Customer Name and Customer Number fields as desired;
  5. Click Save;
  6. A new window will pop up, on it ensure you tick the Devices checkbox for General Changes;
  7. Click Save.
  • The changes will propagate to each device under that group.
