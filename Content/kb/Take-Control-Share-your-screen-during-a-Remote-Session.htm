Take Control: Share your screen during a Remote Session

Last Modified

Thu Apr 18 15:57 GMT 2019

Description

  • How to share your screen with the end user during a remote session?
  • How to reverse a session to show your Desktop to the remote user?

Environment

  • /

Solution

  • During a remote support session:
  1. Click the arrow down icon in the upper right corner;
  2. Press either:
    • Share My Screen (View Only): Displays your Desktop/Screen to the Remote User without allowing them to interact with it;
    • Share My Screen (Keyboard and Mouse): Displays your Desktop/Screen to the Remote User, allowing them to control and interact with it;
  • Note: this is only possible on applet sessions (attended remote support):
    • Agent sessions will show [*] after the session ID in the window title;
    • Applet sessions will show [+] or [-] after the session ID in the window title for elevated permissions or non-elevated permissions respectively.
