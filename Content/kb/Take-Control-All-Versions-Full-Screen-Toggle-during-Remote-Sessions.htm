Take Control (All Versions): Full Screen Toggle during Remote Sessions

Last Modified

Wed Oct 30 15:32 GMT 2019

Description

  • How to switch between Windowed and Full Screen mode in the Remote Desktop tab?

Environment

  • /
    • Take Control

Solution

  1. (If Windowed) Reach to the top bar in the Remote Desktop display/ (If Fullscreen) Hover the mouse on the top border of the window and wait a few seconds until the bar reappears;
  2. Click the Fullscreen toggle button on the top right corner (or use the hotkey Shift + Ctrl + F).
