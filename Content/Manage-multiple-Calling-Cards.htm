Manage one or more Calling Cards

You can manage one or more calling cards from the Admin Area in the Active Cards tab. Select one of the groups from the list to the left to display the calling cards in that group.

  1. Select Management > Calling Cards, select the Calling Card Group you would like to edit, and click Active Cards.
  2. Select the check box corresponding to the card(s) you want to change, and click Edit.
  3. Change the Department, Techs, Duration, or Expiration Date.
  4. Click Save.
