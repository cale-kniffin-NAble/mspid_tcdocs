Provide assistance to your customers

There are multiple ways you can use to interact with your customers, and other technicians in your company. Start a support session to access a customer's computer remotely. Make voice over IP calls when you need to speak directly to a customer or another technician within your company. Access log files for both internal and customer issues, and transfer files during a support session.
