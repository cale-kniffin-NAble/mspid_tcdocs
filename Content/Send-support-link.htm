Send a support link

Similar to the PIN Code, you can copy the Support Link from the console interface and provide it to your customer. They can use the link to initiate a remote support session.

  1. Click Copy to copy the support link to your clipboard, and provide it to your customer
  2. Click EMAIL to open an email in your default mail system with the support link automatically generated.

What do you want to do?

  • Share and transfer support sessions
  • Provide assistance to your customers
