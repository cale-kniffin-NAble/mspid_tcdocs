Chat with customers during a remote session

Perform the following steps to chat with a customer during a remote session:

  1. Navigate to the Chat Icon in the Technician Console to open the Chat Window
  2. Type your message in the Chat area and press Enter to send.

The chat conversation stays open in a new window when navigating away from the general tab. This allows the technician to chat with the customer during the course of the session without navigating between tabs.

If the user receives a message that the technician is not available to chat, click the chat link in the Technician console and click the technician's name to change the User or Technician chat status.

What do you want to do?

  • Chat with potential customers before starting a remote session
  • Chat with colleagues
  • Provide assistance to your customers
