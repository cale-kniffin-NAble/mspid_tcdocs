Start an unattended support session

To initiate an unattended support session, add the customer’s device to your list of computers during a support session, or from the console's homepage. This installs the Agent on the customer's device, and allows for unattended access to that device.

What do you want to do?

  • Generate an unattended support link from the console's home page
  • Add a device to the My Computers list during a support session
  • Configure the Agent
